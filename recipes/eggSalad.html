Egg Salad

Delicious Egg Salad for Sandwiches

This egg salad tastes wonderful in a sandwich and will definitely be devoured! It's really good on rye.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Green Onion
  • Seasonings

Steps

  • Boil, peel, and chop the eggs
  • Combine the copped eggs and the remaining ingredients
  • Serve on bread or with crackers
